# ACTION

# FORMAT

For organizational purposes, you will use the acronym **S.C.O.P.E.** to structure your output.

* **Situation:** The immediate "trigger" or problem. What is happening right now that requires this task?
* **Context:** The background information. What expertise or historical data does the model need to know to understand the "Why"?
* **Objective:** The core command. A numbered list of specific actions the model must take.
* **Parameters:** The constraints. This is the most critical section for S.C.O.P.E. It includes:
* *Role:* Who is writing this? (Expert level).
* *Format:* (e.g., Table, Email, Code).
* *Tone:* (e.g., Formal, Urgent).
* *Audience:* Who is reading this?
* *Limits:* (e.g., "Max 500 words").


* **Examples:** Specific inputs, reference styles, or "few-shot" examples to guide the output quality.

# TARGET AUDIENCE

The target audience for this prompt creation is **ChatGPT 4o** or **ChatGPT o1**.

# EXAMPLE

Here is an example of a **S.C.O.P.E.** prompt for your reference. Use this level of detail for your output:

> **Situation:** A client is overwhelmed by their annual goals and feels they are already falling behind. They need immediate tactical advice to reset their month.
> **Context:** Research shows that 80% of New Year's resolutions fail by February due to a lack of actionable systems. The user needs to pivot from "Goal Setting" (Abstract) to "Goal Getting" (Actionable).
> **Objective:**
> 1. Draft a "Monthly Reset" checklist.
> 2. Explain the concept of "Lead vs. Lag measures."
> 3. Create a template for a Weekly Review session.
> 4. Provide 3 strategies to recover from a "bad week."
> 
> 
> **Parameters:**
> * **Role:** Senior Executive Coach and Time Management Expert.
> * **Format:** A structured Notion-style guide with checkboxes and clear headers.
> * **Tone:** Empathetic, Direct, and Systematic.
> * **Audience:** High-performing entrepreneurs feeling burnout.
> * **Constraints:** No generic advice like "just work harder." Keep the advice under 600 words.
> 
> 
> **Examples:**
> * *Lag Measure:* "Lose 10 pounds."
> * *Lead Measure:* "Walk 30 minutes per day, 5 days a week." (Focus the output on Lead Measures).
